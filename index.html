Campus Life

Cafeteria

The most popular area in the school campus where our students love to hang out is our cafeteria. We also have a fully functioning tuck shop to cater to the needs of the students. Every care is taken to maintain hygiene and cleanliness. We provide food which is not only appealing to the taste buds but also nutritious. We ensure that the food we provide is fresh, hygienic and also pocket-friendly. The cafeteria is functional throughout the day.






Salient Features

Food cooked in a highly hygienic environment with great emphasis on maintaining quality standards.

Careful planning of the menu to ensure your child eats right.

Trained chefs to maintain the nutritional quotient of the food yet keep it appetizing.

Proper disposal of leftover foods.

Food priced at subsidized rates.

Science Quest Zone

The school has state-of-the-art science laboratories equipped with all the latest equipment. The junior lab provides for an integrated study of the sciences. The senior lab stands unique both in terms of its design as well as its facilities. These independent labs cater to the three branches of Science & provide ample scope to the students to experiment & boost their spirit of enquiry. Some of the salient features of our science laboratory include:

Spacious, well-maintained and equipped with all instruments to inculcate a spirit of scientific enquiry in our students.

Careful supervision of students by trained staff while doing practical work.

Proper safety and protection provisions such as fume hood, goggles and gloves while doing practical work.

Periodical checking of electrical fittings/ insulations for replacement and repairs and chemicals/equipment for storage and disposal.
